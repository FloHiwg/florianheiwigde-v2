## Intro

This trip was much more of a classic road trip than our South Africa journey.

Instead of one city and one nature base, this route kept moving. We combined California, several national park areas, Las Vegas, the desert, Yellowstone, and the final coastal return into one long loop across the western United States.

The trip ran from June 13, 2025 to July 13, 2025 and had a very different rhythm depending on the stop. Some places were about hiking and scenery, some were mainly transit pivots, and some worked best as short recovery stops between longer driving days.

In this article, I want to document the route stop by stop, what we booked, how the route actually worked in practice, and what I would keep or change next time.

## Final Verdict

This was an ambitious Western USA round trip with a lot of variety packed into one month.

Its strength was the contrast: alpine scenery, national parks, desert landscapes, Yellowstone, big-city resets, and a final California coast and San Francisco ending.

If I were to recommend a version of this route, I would recommend it to people who genuinely enjoy moving through a trip instead of staying in one place for long. At the same time, I would probably also recommend simplifying parts of it unless the goal is explicitly to see as many different Western USA landscapes as possible in one journey.
