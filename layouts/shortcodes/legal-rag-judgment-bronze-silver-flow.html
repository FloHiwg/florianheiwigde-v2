Step 1
Normalize headings
Repair OCR spacing, strip numbering noise, and map variants like T e n o r or Entscheidungsgründe to known section labels.
Step 2
Detect legal sections
Classify core judgment blocks such as header, operative part, facts, and reasoning, including fallback transition patterns when headings are missing.
Step 3
Chunk by paragraph logic
Accumulate paragraph blocks into retrieval chunks without splitting legal reasoning mid-argument, then attach extracted citation metadata.
