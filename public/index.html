ABOUT ME

My name is Flo and I am a passionate product person. I love to develop products together with a team and let different contexts interact. Getting my hands dirty by acquiring new domain knowledge and understanding new business areas in order to turn them around.

I got in touch with rapid prototyping and lean startup early in my career and product thinking is now in my DNA.

At the same time, compromise is what rapid product development is all about, so I like to communicate and convince people to work together and create compromises and overlaps.

At the same time my real background is software development and I still love to discuss knee deep technical problems and find the perfect solution for the situation together with specialists.

I am the one who builds products!

MY SERVICES
#1
Product

I can help you and your team to build a product and accompany the whole. From problem identification to the finished product. I can also assist in setting up the right methodology for the team and finding the right processes.

#2
Tech

Choosing the right tech stack is difficult for many and finding the right solutions to test things quickly is a red rag for even more. Here I can support you and your team and show you how to quickly test new business models, but also to find the right point at which you should get into real building and move away from testing and working in proof of concepts. Because it is not only about building software, but about developing sustainable software.

#3
Startup

Large companies often have prejudices or at least difficulties working with startups, but many have the desire to match them in terms of speed. I can help with that. Making the gap smaller or even finding ways to achieve the same agility and flexibility yourself. This can range from setting up new formats to support innovation or working directly with innovators to setting up your own innovation departments. I am happy to help!
