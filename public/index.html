ABOUT ME

My name is Flo, and I am a dedicated product professional with a deep passion for creating innovative solutions. I thrive on collaborating with teams to bring ideas to life, seamlessly integrating diverse perspectives and contexts. I'm always eager to dive into new domains, acquiring the knowledge needed to transform business challenges into opportunities.

Early in my career, I embraced rapid prototyping and the lean startup methodology, and these principles have become ingrained in my approach to product development.

I understand that rapid product development often involves finding the right balance between speed and quality. I enjoy the challenge of communicating, negotiating, and building consensus among stakeholders to achieve effective compromises and shared goals.

With a strong foundation in software development, I still relish the opportunity to engage in deep technical discussions, working closely with specialists to identify the perfect solution for each unique situation.

I am the one who builds products and drives them to success!

MY SERVICES
#1
Product

I can support you and your team in building a product from start to finish. From identifying the core problem to delivering the final product, I’ll guide you through each step of the process. I can also help establish the right methodologies and processes tailored to your team’s needs, ensuring a smooth and efficient workflow.

#2
Tech

Choosing the right tech stack can be challenging, and finding quick solutions to test ideas can be even more daunting. I’m here to help you and your team navigate these decisions. I’ll show you how to rapidly test new business models, and more importantly, when it’s time to transition from testing and proof-of-concept stages to full-scale development. My focus isn’t just on building software, but on creating sustainable, scalable solutions.

#3
Startup

Large companies often struggle with the speed and agility of startups, yet many aspire to match their pace. I can help bridge this gap. Whether it's reducing barriers between your company and startups, or enhancing your own innovation capabilities, I’ll work with you to achieve greater agility and flexibility. This can include everything from establishing new innovation formats and collaborating with external innovators to setting up your own innovation departments. I’m here to help you thrive in today’s fast-paced environment!
